School Facilities


Kasagama Secondary school boasts of functional laboratories for Chemistry and Biology, a Physics and Agriculture lab, ICT facilities with internet connectivity, spacious dormitories and classrooms.


Dormitories


Our students are accomodated in spacious dormitories. Burglar proofing has been removed to make them compliant with the Ministry of Education Safrty Standards. Students beds are single or double decked to reduce risks of accidents and to align the facilities to required boarding section Standards.

Environment


Kasagama Secondary school sits on vast land. Learners are accorded a quiet environment to read, attend lessons, and revise without urban incoveniences.

Library


Our library is fully stocked with textbooks, references books, networked computers for research. Our book-to-student ratio is 1:1. This was achieved as a result of funding from the Government through the Ministry of Education and Sports. We encourage students to buy personal novels and plays. this, we hope, will help to boost their composition and comprehension skills.
