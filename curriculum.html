Skills Based Curriculum


At Kasagama Secondary School, we are committed to providing quality instruction and a supportive learning environment that empowers our students to reach their full academic potential under the framework of the New Lower Secondary Curriculum. We believe that this curriculum will not only prepare them for academic success but also equip them with the skills and competencies necessary to become responsible and productive members of society.


Assessment and Progression


Students in Senior 4 will undertake the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations, which have been reformed to align with the principles of the New Lower Secondary Curriculum. Our dedicated teachers provide comprehensive preparation to ensure students are well-equipped for these assessments.

Scope of Curriculum



Languages: English, Kiswahili, and local languages.

Mathematics: Fossing on conceptual understanding and application.

Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, emphasizing practical investigation and scientific inquiry.

Humanities: History, Geography, fostering an understanding of society and responsible citizenship.

Religious Education: Promoting ethical and moral development.

Creative Arts: Music, Dance, Drama, and Art, nurturing creativity and self-expression.

Technology and Enterprise: Equipping students with digital skills and an entrepreneurial mindset.

Physical Education: Promoting health and well-being through physical activity.
