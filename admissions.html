Admission to Kasagama Secondary School


Welcome to Kasagama Secondary School, a center of academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. We are delighted that you are considering joining our vibrant school community. Admission is open to students who are ready to grow intellectually, socially, and morally.


Admission Requirements


For Senior One (S.1):

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) results slip or certificate.
  • Completed application form (available at the school or downloaded from our website).
  • Two passport-size photographs.
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID (if available).
  • Admission and medical forms duly filled by the parent/guardian.


For Senior Five (S.5):

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) results slip or certificate.
  • Recommendation letter from the former school (for transfers).
  • Completed application and medical forms.
  • Two passport-size photographs.
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID.
