Games and Sports at Kasagama Secondary School


At Kasagama Secondary School, we believe in the importance of physical education and the development of students through games and sports. Our sports program is designed to promote discipline, teamwork, leadership, fitness, and a healthy competitive spirit among learners.

Competitions and Achievements


Students actively compete in Inter-House Competitionss, District and Zonal Competitions, and National Secondary Schools Championships. The school holds an annual Sports Day, where students showcase their talents in various disciplines. Parents, old students, and community members are invited to cheer and celebrate student athletes.


All students are encouraged to participate in at least one sporting activity. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, there is a place for you to grow and shine at Kasagama Secondary School.
