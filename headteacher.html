Headteacher's Message


Dear All,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Kasagama Secondary School. As Headteacher, I am proud to lead a school that is deeply committed to nurturing excellence, discipline, and holistic development in every learner.

At Kasagama S.S., we believe that education is not only about academic success but also about building character, instilling values, and preparing our students for the challenges of the future. Our dedicated staff work tirelessly to create a supportive and inspiring learning environment where each student is encouraged to reach their full potential.

We are grateful to our parents, the Board of Governors, the community, and all stakeholders for their continued support. Together, we are shaping a generation of responsible, self-driven, and innovative individuals.

I invite you to explore our website and discover the rich academic and co-curricular life at Kasagama Secondary School. Let us continue to work hand in hand to raise the standard of education and uphold the values that define our school.

Ssenfuma Herbert Mitti,
Headteacher/B.O.G Secretary
