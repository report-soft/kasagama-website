Our Curriculum


Our academic program for Senior 1 to Senior 4 is designed to equip students with the essential knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in the 21st century.

The New Curriculum emphasizes thematic learning, where subjects are integrated to provide a deeper and more meaningful understanding of concepts. This approach encourages students to make connections across disciplines and apply their learning to real-world contexts.


Games and Sports


We believe in the importance of physical education and the development of students through games and sports. Our sports program is designed to promote discipline, teamwork, leadership, fitness, and a healthy competitive spirit among learners.
