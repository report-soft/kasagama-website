About Kasagama Secondary School


Welcome to Kasagama Secondary School, a Government Aided institution located in Lyantonde District, in Kasagama sub-county. The school started as a private community school with a vision to provide a holistic education to Kasagama and surrounding communities. It was later acquired by the Government and modern facilites built to accomodate the increasing enrollment. The school currently has an enrollment of 412 students


At Kasagama Secondary School, we aspire to nurture individuals who not only excel academically but also possess the moral and ethical foundation to make a positive impact on society.


Our students have consistently excelled in National Examinations, a trend we attribute to conducive learning environment and the good relationship between the school, parents and the community. We are committed to producing well-rounded individuals full of integrity, empathy, compassion, and hardwork.


Our Vision


To be the leading education service provider in Uganda and beyond whose products have a balanced Holistic approach , positive attitude towards work, creative and proactive.

Our Mission


To produce all round students who are skilled, versatile and can fit in any good society wherever they go.
