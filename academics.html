Our Curriculum


At Kasagama Secondary School, we are dedicated to providing a dynamic and relevant academic experience for all our students. As a forward-thinking institution, we have fully embraced the New Lower Secondary Curriculum implemented by the Ugandan Ministry of Education and Sports. This learner-centered approach focuses on developing competencies, fostering critical thinking, and promoting holistic growth among our students.


Our academic program for Senior 1 to Senior 4 is designed to equip students with the essential knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in the 21st century. The New Curriculum emphasizes thematic learning, where subjects are integrated to provide a deeper and more meaningful understanding of concepts. This approach encourages students to make connections across disciplines and apply their learning to real-world contexts.


Competency-Based Learning


Our teaching methodologies prioritize the development of key competencies such as critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, communication, collaboration, and digital literacy.

Student-Centered Approach


We believe in active learning where students are at the heart of the learning process. Our teachers facilitate learning through inquiry-based activities, group work, projects, and presentations, encouraging student engagement and ownership of their learning.

Continuous Assessment


The New Curriculum emphasizes continuous assessment, which involves a variety of methods to track student progress throughout the term. This includes classroom-based assessments, projects, and practical activities, providing timely feedback to both students and teachers.

Subject Areas


Our curriculum covers a broad range of subjects designed to cater to diverse interests and future pathways. These include

Sciences (Mathematics, Biology, Physics, Chemistry);

Humanities (History, Geography, CRE, Literature in English, English Language);

Local Languages (Luganda, RR);

Vocational & Business (Entrepreneurship Education, Agricultural, Art & Design, ICT)
